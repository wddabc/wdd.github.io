I'm a PhD candidate (ABD) working with Prof. Jason Eisner on natural language processing and computational linguistics. The main goal is making unsupervised structured learning more tractable on natural language.

I received my M.S. in Computer Science from Columbia University advised by Prof. Michael Collins and Dr. Rebecca Passonneau. I received my B.Eng in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University advised by Prof. Yong Yu. I'm also an alumnus of ACM Honored Class. See my CV for more details.
